Brand Voice

✓ Do — Operator tone
"73% faster response. 4 tools → 1. No app for guests." Short. Specific. Operator language.
✗ Don't — Corporate fluff
"Attenda leverages cutting-edge AI to synergistically optimize your hospitality ecosystem." Never.
✓ Do — Real numbers
"One hotel, Fort Lauderdale, 121 rooms — earned $7,820 from cruise-day shuttle in 4 months. Here's how."
✗ Don't — Vague claims
"Attenda helps hotels increase revenue and improve guest satisfaction across all touchpoints." Too broad.
✓ Do — Name the pain
"It's 7am. Guest wants towels. Housekeeper on radio 2. Front desk on 3. Neither picks up. That was my hotel in 2019."
✗ Don't — Feature lists
"Key features include: real-time messaging, task management, shuttle scheduling, KPI tracking, and more!"

Logo Usage

✓ Do
✓ Use on white or very light backgrounds
✓ Use white version on teal or dark navy
✓ Maintain clear space equal to the icon width on all sides
✓ Use icon mark alone at small sizes (under 120px wide)
✓ Keep wordmark lowercase — "attenda" not "Attenda"
✗ Don't
✗ Recolor the icon to anything other than teal or white
✗ Stretch or distort the logo proportions
✗ Place on busy photography or low-contrast backgrounds
✗ Add drop shadows or effects to the logo
✗ Use the wordmark without the icon in formal contexts
