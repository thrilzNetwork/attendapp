# <span style="color: #7B1C3E;">SOP-MG-01 — MORNING BRIEF REVIEW</span>

---

**Steps:**

1. Log in at `attendaapp.com/staff`.
2. Review the **Dashboard** — check GM Notes left by the previous shift or GM.
3. Note **Pending Requests** — any items open over 30 minutes need follow-up.
4. Check the **Callouts** tab badge — if any pending callouts exist, handle them before the shift begins (SOP-MG-02).
5. Confirm the **Shuttle Times** section shows today's runs correctly.
6. Post a GM Note if you are duty manager and have shift-relevant information to share.

# <span style="color: #7B1C3E;">SOP-MG-02 — CALLOUT MANAGEMENT</span>

---

**Purpose:** Handle staff callouts and coordinate coverage.

**Access:** Callouts tab (badge shows pending count)

### Reviewing Callouts

1. Click **Callouts** — the badge number shows pending items.
2. Each callout shows: staff name, date, reason (Sick/Personal/Family Emergency/Other), affected shift.

### Approving Without Coverage

Use when the shift can absorb the absence or is already covered:

1. Click **Approve** on the callout card.
2. The callout is acknowledged and removed from the pending count.

### Finding Coverage

Use when the shift needs to be filled:

1. Click **Find Cover** on the callout card.
2. The Schedules view opens filtered to the callout date.
3. Identify which staff are available (not already scheduled for that day).
4. Click **+ Add Shift** and schedule the covering staff member.
5. Return to Callouts and click **Approve** to close the callout.

> **Visual aid:** A red dot on the Schedules tab shows which dates have open callouts — useful during weekly schedule review.

# <span style="color: #7B1C3E;">SOP-MG-03 — SCHEDULE MANAGEMENT</span>

---

**Purpose:** Build, maintain, and publish weekly staff schedules.

**Access:** Operations → Schedules

### Building a New Week

1. Click **Schedules** and navigate to the target week using **← →** arrows.
2. Click **+ Add Shift** for each shift to add:
   - Select staff member (role auto-fills from their profile)
   - Set date, start time, end time
   - Add notes if needed
   - For housekeeping: check **Open end time** if end time is unknown
3. Repeat for all shifts in the week.

### Editing Existing Shifts

1. Click any shift card on the grid.
2. The **Edit Shift** modal opens with all current values pre-filled.
3. Modify as needed → click **Save Changes**.
4. To remove: click **Delete Shift** (red button, bottom of modal).

### Copying Last Week's Schedule

1. Navigate to the week you want to copy from.
2. Click **Copy to Next Week**.
3. Confirm if prompted (next week already has shifts).
4. All shifts are duplicated 7 days forward — review and adjust before publishing.

### Using Schedule Templates

**Save current week as template:**
1. Click **Save as Template** (bookmark icon in header).
2. Name the template (e.g., "Low Season Week", "Holiday Staffing").
3. Click Save.

**Apply a template to a new week:**
1. Navigate to the target week.
2. Click **Load Template** (folder icon).
3. Select a template → **Apply**.
4. Review and edit the generated shifts before publishing.

### Publishing the Schedule

1. Review all shifts for the week.
2. Click **Publish** — staff can now view their schedule.
3. Communicate schedule availability to staff per hotel policy.

# <span style="color: #7B1C3E;">SOP-MG-04 — HOUSEKEEPING TBD SHIFTS</span>

---

**Purpose:** Set the actual end time for housekeeping shifts that were created with open (TBD) end times.

**Access:** Operations → Schedules

### Setting the End Time at Day's End

1. Navigate to **Schedules** and locate today's date.
2. Housekeeping shifts with unknown end times display `→ TBD` in **amber**.
3. Click the **→ TBD** badge on the shift card.
4. A small popover appears with a time input.
5. Enter the actual end time.
6. Click **Set** — the shift updates immediately and the amber indicator clears.

> **Best practice:** Do this for all housekeeping staff at the end of the shift before the supervisor logs off.

# <span style="color: #7B1C3E;">SOP-MG-05 — POSITION TO-DO REVIEW</span>

---

**Purpose:** Confirm all positions completed their daily checklists.

**Access:** Operations → Position To-Dos

### Reviewing Completion Status

1. Click **Position To-Dos** in the Operations section.
2. The **Completion Board** shows all active positions with completion percentages.
3. Click any position to see which specific items were completed and which are pending.
4. If a position is incomplete near shift end, follow up with the responsible staff member.

### Managing To-Do Items (Manager/Admin)

1. In Position To-Dos, click **Manage** (visible to Manager/Admin).
2. To add an item: click **+ New Item**, fill in task, position, frequency, and category → Save.
3. To deactivate an item: toggle the Active switch off.

### Installing To-Do Packs from Marketplace

1. Navigate to **Marketplace**.
2. Browse To-Do Packs (e.g., "Front Desk Opening Checklist").
3. Click **Install** — items are added to the relevant position's checklist immediately.

# <span style="color: #7B1C3E;">SOP-MG-06 — FORECAST SUBMISSION</span>

---

**Purpose:** Submit daily occupancy forecast to drive staffing recommendations.

**Access:** Operations → Forecast

### Submitting a Forecast

1. Click **Forecast** in Operations.
2. Click the target date on the monthly calendar (or **New Forecast**).
3. Fill in:
   - Expected Occupancy (rooms)
   - Arrivals
   - Departures
   - Group Blocks
   - VIP Arrivals
   - Notes (events, demand context)
4. Click **Submit Forecast**.
5. Optional: click **Generate Shifts** — the system suggests shifts based on occupancy ratios. Review before accepting.

# <span style="color: #7B1C3E;">SOP-MG-07 — RIGHT ANSWERS APPROVAL</span>

---

**Purpose:** Review and approve staff-submitted knowledge base suggestions.

**Access:** Operations → Right Answers → Pending

### Approving a Submission

1. Click **Right Answers** in Operations.
2. Click the **Pending** tab.
3. Read the staff member's submitted situation + suggested response.
4. Edit the response text if needed.
5. Click **Approve** → the item is added to the live knowledge base.
6. Click **Reject** → optionally add feedback for the staff member.
