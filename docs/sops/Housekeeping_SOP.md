# <span style="color: #7B1C3E;">SOP-HK-01 — LOGGING IN</span>

---

**Steps:**

1. Open a browser and go to `attendaapp.com/staff`.

# <span style="color: #7B1C3E;">SOP-HK-02 — VIEWING YOUR SCHEDULE</span>

---

**Purpose:** Check your upcoming shifts.

**Access:** Operations → Schedules

**Steps:**

1. Click **Schedules** in the Operations section.
2. The schedule grid shows the current week.
3. Your shifts are highlighted with your name.
4. Use **← →** arrows to view future weeks.

> **TBD shifts:** If your shift card shows `→ TBD` in amber, your supervisor has not yet set the end time. Your start time is confirmed — check with your supervisor for expected end time.

---

# <span style="color: #7B1C3E;">SOP-HK-03 — POSITION TO-DO CHECKLIST</span>

---

**Purpose:** Complete your assigned daily task checklist for your position.

**Access:** Operations → Position To-Dos

### Completing Your Daily Checklist

1. Click **Position To-Dos** in the Operations section.
2. Your position's checklist for today loads automatically.
3. As you complete each task, tap/click the checkbox next to it.
4. Each item is timestamped when checked.
5. When all items are complete, click **Submit Checklist**.

> **Important:** Complete the checklist during or immediately after performing each task — do not wait until the end of your shift to check everything off at once. Timestamps are used by supervisors to track progress.

### What If a Task Cannot Be Completed?

1. Leave the item unchecked.
2. Add a note in the comments field (if available) explaining why.
3. Report it verbally to your supervisor before shift end.

# <span style="color: #7B1C3E;">SOP-HK-04 — HANDLING HOUSEKEEPING REQUESTS</span>

---

**Purpose:** Respond to guest housekeeping requests logged through Attenda.

**Access:** Today → Requests tab

### Viewing Housekeeping Requests

1. Click **Requests** in the Today section.
2. Filter by type if needed — look for **Housekeeping** requests (broom icon).
3. Requests appear with: guest name, room number, request description, and time since submission.

### Accepting a Request

1. Click the request card.
2. Click **Acknowledge** — confirms you have seen and are handling the request.
3. The card color changes from amber to blue.

### Completing a Request

1. After fulfilling the request (extra towels delivered, room serviced, etc.):
2. Click the request card.
3. Click **Complete** — the card turns green and is timestamped.

> **Tip:** Complete requests in Attenda the moment the physical task is done. Guests can see their request status, so prompt completion improves the guest experience.

---

# <span style="color: #7B1C3E;">SOP-HK-05 — SUBMITTING HOUSEKEEPING KPIs</span>

---

**Purpose:** Enter daily housekeeping performance data.

**Access:** Operations → KPIs → Housekeeping

### Submitting KPIs

1. Click **KPIs** in the Operations section.
2. Click the **Housekeeping** category tab.
3. Enter values for each metric assigned to your role (e.g., Rooms Cleaned, CPOR, Inspection Score).
4. Click **Submit** — a green checkmark confirms the submission.

> **When:** Submit at the end of your shift, or as directed by your supervisor.

---

# <span style="color: #7B1C3E;">SOP-HK-06 — REPORTING A CALLOUT</span>

---

**Purpose:** Formally notify management that you cannot make your scheduled shift.

**Access:** Callouts tab

**Steps:**

1. Click **Callouts** in the left navigation.
2. Click **Report Callout**.
3. Fill in:

| Field | Options |
|-------|---------|
| Shift Date | The date you cannot work |
| Reason | Sick / Personal / Family Emergency / Other |
| Notes | Any additional context |

4. Click **Submit** — your supervisor is notified immediately in the Callouts tab.

> ⚠️ **Also call your supervisor directly** per hotel policy. The Attenda callout is the official record, but a phone call is still required.

---

# <span style="color: #7B1C3E;">SOP-HK-07 — LEARNING & HR</span>

---

**Purpose:** Complete assigned training courses and acknowledge HR documents.

**Access:** Learning & HR tab

### Completing a Training Course

1. Click **Learning & HR** in the left navigation.
2. Click the **Courses** tab.
3. Open an assigned course.
4. Read or view each module in order.
5. At the end, complete the **Quiz** (multiple choice, 80% to pass).
6. A **Certificate of Completion** is awarded on passing.

### Acknowledging HR Documents

1. In Learning & HR, click the **Documents** tab.
2. Look for documents marked **"Requires Acknowledgement"** (highlighted).
3. Read the document.
4. Click **I Acknowledge** — records that you have read it.

---

# <span style="color: #7B1C3E;">SOP-HK-08 — CULTURE HUB</span>

---

**Purpose:** Check the team leaderboard and redeem reward points.

**Access:** Operations → Culture Hub

### Viewing the Leaderboard

1. Click **Culture Hub** in Operations.
2. The **Leaderboard** tab shows the monthly points ranking.
3. Your position and point total are shown.

### Redeeming Points

1. Click the **Rewards** tab in Culture Hub.
2. Browse available rewards and their point costs.
3. Click **Redeem** on a reward you have enough points for.
4. Your supervisor will approve or deny the redemption.
