# <span style="color: #7B1C3E;">SOP-ST-01 — LOGGING IN</span>

---

**Steps:**

1. Open a browser on any device and go to `attendaapp.com/staff`.
2. Enter your **email** and **password**.
3. Click **Sign In**.
4. The Dashboard loads — you're in.

> **First time logging in?** Your manager set a temporary password. Change it immediately:
> 1. Click your name in the top-right corner.
> 2. Select **Account Settings**.
> 3. Update your password and click **Save**.

# <span style="color: #7B1C3E;">SOP-ST-02 — VIEWING YOUR SCHEDULE</span>

---

**Purpose:** See your upcoming shifts.

**Access:** Operations → Schedules

**Steps:**

1. Click **Schedules** in the Operations section.
2. The weekly schedule grid shows the current week.
3. Locate your name in the left column — your shifts are shown in each day column.
4. Use **← →** arrows to navigate to future or past weeks.

> **Published schedules only:** You can only see your schedule after a manager has published it. If your schedule is blank, ask your manager if the week has been published yet.

---

# <span style="color: #7B1C3E;">SOP-ST-03 — REPORTING A CALLOUT</span>

---

**Purpose:** Formally notify management that you cannot attend your scheduled shift.

**Access:** Callouts tab

**Steps:**

1. Click **Callouts** in the left navigation.
2. Click **Report Callout**.
3. Fill in the form:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Shift Date** | The date of the shift you're missing |
| **Reason** | Sick / Personal / Family Emergency / Other |
| **Notes** | Optional — any additional context |

4. Click **Submit** — your manager sees it immediately in the Callouts tab.

> ⚠️ **Attenda does not replace calling your manager.** Per hotel policy, you must also call or text your direct supervisor. Attenda creates the official record; the phone call is the notification.

**Viewing your past callouts:**
- In the Callouts tab, scroll below the Report button to see your callout history and whether each was approved.

# <span style="color: #7B1C3E;">SOP-ST-04 — SUBMITTING KPIs</span>

---

**Purpose:** Enter your daily performance data for your department.

**Access:** Operations → KPIs

**Steps:**

1. Click **KPIs** in the Operations section.
2. Click on your department's category tab.
3. Enter values for each metric in the list.
4. Click **Submit** — a green checkmark confirms it was saved.

> **When to submit:** At the end of your shift or at the time your manager specifies. Each category can only be submitted once per day per person.

# <span style="color: #7B1C3E;">SOP-ST-05 — POSITION TO-DO CHECKLIST</span>

---

**Purpose:** Complete and log your daily position tasks.

**Access:** Operations → Position To-Dos

**Steps:**

1. Click **Position To-Dos** in Operations.
2. Today's checklist for your position loads automatically.
3. Complete each task physically, then check it off in Attenda.
4. When all items are done, click **Submit Checklist**.

> **Each checkbox is timestamped** when you check it — be honest. Do not check off tasks you haven't done.

# <span style="color: #7B1C3E;">SOP-ST-06 — RIGHT ANSWERS (KNOWLEDGE BASE)</span>

---

**Purpose:** Get instant guidance on how to handle guest situations professionally.

**Access:** Operations → Right Answers

### Searching for an Answer

1. Click **Right Answers** in Operations.
2. Type keywords describing the situation in the search bar.

   *Examples:* "guest noise complaint", "billing dispute", "broken AC", "lost and found"

3. Matching knowledge base articles appear ranked by relevance.
4. Read the **Approved Response** — use it as your guide for what to say or do.

### Getting an AI Suggestion

If no KB article matches your situation:

1. After searching, click **Get AI Suggestion**.
2. Wait 2–5 seconds — a suggested professional response appears.
3. Use the suggestion as a starting point and adapt to your specific situation.

> **AI suggestions are not official policy.** If you're unsure, escalate to a manager before responding to the guest.

### Submitting a New Suggestion

If you handle a situation well and think others could benefit:

1. Click **Suggest Answer** on the search results screen.
2. Describe the situation and your suggested staff response.
3. Click **Submit** — a manager will review and approve it for the knowledge base.

# <span style="color: #7B1C3E;">SOP-ST-07 — LEARNING & HR</span>

---

**Purpose:** Complete training and acknowledge HR documents.

**Access:** Learning & HR tab

### Completing a Training Course

1. Click **Learning & HR** in the left navigation.
2. Click the **Courses** tab.
3. Open an assigned course — click the first module.
4. Read through each module in order, then click **Next Module**.
5. Complete the **Quiz** at the end (multiple choice, must score 80% to pass).
6. A **Certificate of Completion** is issued automatically on passing.

> **Failed the quiz?** You can retake it. Review the modules before trying again.

### Acknowledging HR Documents

1. In Learning & HR, click the **Documents** tab.
2. Documents with **"Requires Acknowledgement"** are highlighted.
3. Open and read the document.
4. Click **I Acknowledge** at the bottom — this records your confirmation.

# <span style="color: #7B1C3E;">SOP-ST-08 — CULTURE HUB</span>

---

**Purpose:** Track your points, see where you stand on the team leaderboard, and redeem rewards.

**Access:** Operations → Culture Hub

### Viewing the Leaderboard

1. Click **Culture Hub** in Operations.
2. The Leaderboard shows all staff ranked by monthly points.
3. Your name, rank, and points are highlighted.

**How to earn points:**
- Submitting KPIs on time
- Completing training courses
- Receiving positive guest feedback (logged by a manager)
- Attendance and punctuality

### Redeeming Points for Rewards

1. In Culture Hub, click the **Rewards** tab.
2. Browse the catalog — each reward shows the point cost.
3. Click **Redeem** on a reward you can afford (points ≥ cost).
4. Your manager reviews and approves or denies the request.

### Checking Team Events

1. In Culture Hub, click the **Events** tab.
2. Upcoming team meetings, outings, or celebrations are listed here.
3. RSVP if the event has an attendance option.
